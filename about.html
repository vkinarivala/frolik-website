Who we are

A small Ahmedabad studio that designs like it's listening.

We're a founder-led interior design studio, small on purpose. That means the person you meet in the first conversation is the same person thinking about your storage, your light and your budget all the way to the last cushion — and it means we take on the kind of work we can give our full attention.

We came to interiors the long way round — through a love of how ordinary rooms shape ordinary days. A good home isn't the one that photographs best; it's the one that makes a Tuesday morning easier. So we spend our first hours listening: to routines, to the things that aren't working, to the small rituals a family would never think to mention but would miss if they were gone.

From there we design homes, workspaces and commercial spaces across the city — warm, functional, and full of personality. And along the way we became known for one thing in particular: rooms for children that parents don't have to apologise for.

Our soft spot

Why children's rooms became a signature.

A children's room asks more of a designer than almost anything else. It has to be playful and calm, safe and free, structured enough to study in and soft enough to sleep in — and it has to keep working as the child outgrows the version of themselves it was built for.

Solving that well taught us to plan play, study, sleep, storage and safety as one connected world, not a theme pasted on afterwards. Parents started to notice, and word travelled. It's become the work we're best known for.

We're still a full-service studio — homes, workspaces and commercial spaces are the heart of what we do. Kids' spaces simply gave us a way of thinking that makes every space we touch a little more thoughtful.

Where we work

Rooted in Ahmedabad.

The studio is based in Ahmedabad, and the city is written into how we work — its light, its craft traditions, the way homes here are built to hold big families and busy days. Most of our projects are within the city and its neighbourhoods, close enough to visit a site on a moment's notice.

We take on homes, kids' spaces, workspaces and commercial interiors across Ahmedabad and around Gujarat — and we've travelled a little further for the right project. If your space is somewhere else, tell us where; we'll be honest about how we can help.
