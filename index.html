How it works

Thoughtful from the first sketch
to the final cushion.

Every project begins with how the space will be used — routines, light, storage, movement, materials and mood before the look.

1

Discovery

A site visit and a real conversation — lifestyle, family needs, budget and timeline before anything else.

2

Concept

Mood board, colour story, zoning and a rough direction — argued honestly until it feels like yours.

3

Design

Layouts, furniture, materials, lighting and storage — with 3D views so you can see it before it's built.

4

Build

Custom furniture, vendor coordination and site work — we manage the hundred tiny decisions so you keep your evenings.

5

Style & handover

Styling, photographs and a final walkthrough — down to the last bookshelf, then we get out of your way.
