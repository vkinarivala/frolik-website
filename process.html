Every project begins with how the space will be used - we study routines, light, storage, movement, materials and mood before we design the look.

Before the look

We design the life first, then the room.

We start with how you'll actually use the space - the routines that repeat every day, where the light lands from morning to evening, what needs to be stored and hidden, how people move between rooms, and the mood you want to feel when you walk in. Get those right and the look almost designs itself. It's why the prettiest room on a mood board isn't the goal - the room that fits your life is.

The process

Discovery to handover, in five steps.

The same considered arc on every project - so an unknown starts to feel planned from the very first call.

1

Discovery

A site visit and a real conversation - lifestyle, family and children's needs, budget and timeline. We measure the space, note the light and quirks, and listen to how you actually live before a single line is drawn.

2

Concept

Mood board, references, colour story, zoning and a rough direction. We put a clear point of view in front of you early - so we can argue it honestly together and adjust before it costs anything to change.

3

Design

Layouts, furniture, materials, lighting and storage resolved down to the detail - with 3D views where they help, so you can see and approve the room before it's built rather than imagine it.

4

Build

Custom furniture, vendor coordination and site work. We manage the hundred tiny on-site decisions and keep the makers on brief and on schedule, so the finished room matches the drawings you signed off.

5

Style & handover

Styling, photographs and a final walkthrough - down to the last bookshelf and cushion. We snag anything unfinished, hand over the space that's ready to live in, then get out of your way.

What to expect

How involved you'll be.

Enough to feel it's yours, not so much that it takes over your calendar. Discovery is one visit and a longer conversation. Concept and design usually run over a few weeks , with a handful of review sessions where the real choices are made - layout, palette, key furniture and finishes. We bring options with a recommendation; you decide.

Once build starts, we coordinate the makers, vendors and site so you don't have to chase them. Expect a steady rhythm of updates rather than daily calls - a check-in roughly each week and a shout whenever a decision genuinely needs you. Build length depends on scope , and we'll give you an honest timeline in the estimate before anyone lifts a tool.
