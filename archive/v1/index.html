How we work

Thoughtful from the first sketch to the final cushion.

Every project begins with how the space will be used. We study routines, light, storage, movement, materials, and mood before we design the look.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Site, lifestyle, family needs, budget, timeline.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Mood, colour story, zoning, rough direction.

  3. 03

    Design

    Layouts, furniture, materials, lighting, storage.

  4. 04

    Build

    Custom furniture, vendors, site coordination.

  5. 05

    Style & Handover

    Styling, final walkthrough, and the keys.
