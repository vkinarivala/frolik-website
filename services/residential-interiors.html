Before you ask

Home interiors, answered.

How much does a home interior project cost in Ahmedabad?

It depends on the size of the home, the amount of custom joinery and the finish level — a focused apartment refresh sits very differently from a full bungalow fit-out. We give a budget estimate early, during concept, so you can steer the design against a real number rather than a surprise at the end.

How long does a full home take?

Design usually runs six to ten weeks, and build depends on scope — often three to five months for a full home. We share a timeline at the start and keep you updated at each stage so the schedule stays honest.

Do you handle execution, or only the design?

Both. Some clients want the drawings and direction and build it themselves; most ask us to build, coordinate every vendor and hand over a finished home. You choose, and we scope the engagement around it.

Can you work with an existing home or a renovation?

Yes. Renovations and part-home projects are a big part of what we do — we plan around what stays, what changes and how you live in the space today.
