For parents

Kids' rooms, answered.

How much does a kids' room cost in Ahmedabad?

Most of the cost sits in the custom furniture — the bed, the storage, the study unit — so it varies with how much is built-in and the finish you choose. We give a budget estimate at concept stage so you can decide what's worth investing in and what can wait.

Can you design a room that grows with the child?

Yes — that's central to how we plan kids' spaces. We use a calm base palette, adaptable storage and furniture that can be re-styled rather than replaced, so the room still fits a few years from now instead of dating with a theme.

Can you design a shared room for two siblings?

Absolutely. Shared sibling rooms are one of our favourite briefs — we zone sleep, study and play so each child gets a sense of their own corner while the room still reads as one space.

Do you handle the furniture and execution too?

We can. You can take our design and build it, or we can build the custom furniture, coordinate vendors, style the room and hand it over ready to use.
